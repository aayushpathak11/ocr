# OCR

An OCR app that can recognize texts on images.

This app is based on <a href="https://github.com/tesseract-ocr/tesseract/blob/master/README.md">Tesseract 4 </a> and the first of which based on Tesseract 4.
After downloading the Training data, the app does everything offline.

Translate this app on [Hosted Weblate](https://hosted.weblate.org/projects/android-ocr).
